# ResearchOS vs AutoResearchClaw

## Positioning

AutoResearchClaw and ResearchOS are not trying to maximize the same thing.

- AutoResearchClaw pushes toward broader autonomous research automation.
- ResearchOS pushes toward trustworthy research execution.

That distinction matters because research teams do not only need more automation. They also need flow control, metric grounding, recoverability, and operator-visible accountability.

## Short Comparison

| Dimension | AutoResearchClaw | ResearchOS |
|---|---|---|
| Main story | Autonomous research pipeline | Trustworthy research execution system |
| Primary strength | Automation breadth and external agent ecosystem | Auditable flow control and operator visibility |
| Workflow model | Multi-stage automated pipeline | Typed state machine with gate, rollback, retry, pause, resume, pivot, refine |
| Experiment loop | Strong automation narrative | Diagnosis-driven repair plus best-result promotion |
| Draft integrity | Strong generation story | Verified metrics registry blocks unsupported numbers |
| Human role | Can supervise automation | First-class checkpoints and operator control plane |
| Product center | Agent orchestration | Research operating system |

## Where AutoResearchClaw Is Strong

- broader external-agent and platform packaging
- stronger public showcase narrative
- more visible ecosystem story around autonomous execution

## Where ResearchOS Is Strong

- typed workflow state instead of implicit chaining
- checkpoint-aware resume and rollback surfaces
- verified metrics grounding before writing
- branch comparison and event-stream inspection
- stronger operator console story for human-AI collaboration

## How To Explain The Difference Publicly

### Recommended framing

AutoResearchClaw pushes research automation forward. ResearchOS focuses on a different frontier: trustworthy research execution with explicit flow control, metric grounding, checkpoint recovery, and operator visibility.

### Avoid this framing

Do not frame ResearchOS as “the same thing, but with more agents.”

That weakens the product story and collapses your strongest differentiator.

## One-Sentence Summary

AutoResearchClaw aims to automate research more completely. ResearchOS aims to make research automation more trustworthy, inspectable, and controllable.
